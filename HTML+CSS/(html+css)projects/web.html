INTERIOR DESIGN AND CONSULTATION

Karma focus on providing the superior service of interior designers in Delhi. The overall interiors of a space can set an atmosphere, a mood, or give people an impression of their persona about their lifestyle. The first part of interior design process at karma always start with initial space planning in detail by taking into consideration all the essential requisites and concerns that our client’s brings to the table.

To form the direction of your project, we analyze concepts, develop designs and clinch the best way to reach your desired goals. After a concept is established, our team develops the idea of your space further. We provide you with detailed set of drawings which may include furniture layouts, ceiling designs, electrical, wood work, wall concepts and flooring details along with conceptualized rendering for your better understanding.

Throughout the process we keep a sharp, detailed and precise quality check at sites. The final outcome is always original and custom depicting the client’s personality and their feelings about life. Karma works with you on every step of the way to create functional, creative and productive living spaces that works in harmony with your needs and your lifestyle without compromising on quality.

INTERIOR DESIGN AND BUILD

Karma offer complete interior design services in Gurgaon and Delhi. Karma ensures a tenacious, consistent, polished, smooth and seamless process when envisioning the look of our client’s home. Our personalized and committed approach ensures that everything we suggest will fit perfectly within your existing furnishings and space.

Our complete design solution includes space planning, designing, build and install along with finishes, furnishers & fixtures, fine art advisory keeping in mind the detailed pre-decided schedules along with complete design management services, continuous client interaction & aesthetic updates, procurement & project turnover. The integration of colors, furniture, lighting, fixtures, flooring and wall covering are essentials in creating a desired outcome.

Functionality, creativity, quality and elegance are our long-standing design principles. We do everything to meet our client’s expectations or more. With craft, detail attention and consideration we deliver lifelong value and relationships to our esteemed clients.
